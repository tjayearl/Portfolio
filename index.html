About Me

My journey into software engineering began with a fascination for how things work, which quickly evolved into a passion for building digital experiences. I thrive on the challenge of turning a complex problem into a clean, elegant, and user-friendly solution. For me, coding is a creative outlet—a way to bring ideas to life and build tangible products that people can interact with and enjoy.

What sets me apart is my blend of technical skill and a storyteller's mindset. I don't just write code; I build narratives that guide users and solve real-world problems. My background from Moringa School provided a strong foundation, but my drive to continuously learn and adapt is what fuels my growth.
